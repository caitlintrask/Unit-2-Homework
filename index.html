About Me

Hi! My name is Caitlin Trask. I am 26-years-old living in the lovely City Park neighborhood of Denver, CO! I recently got a great new job at VF Corporation as an eCommerce Merchandiser for the JanSport brand. I am about 6 weeks into my job and am loving it so far.

In my free time, I love to be outdoors, try new breweries with friends, shop (a little too much), and binge my favorite online series. Currently can’t get enough of Yellowstone and The Great British Baking Show!

I’m excited to learn coding as it’s going to give me such a leg-up in my career and possibly an entire new career path! I am particularly interested in web design and application development. I’d love you to take a look at my portfolio and see what I’ve created so far!
